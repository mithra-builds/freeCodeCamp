Chocolate Cake

This is a popular, moist one-bowl chocolate cake recipe that requires 2 cups sugar, 1-3/4 cups flour, 3/4 cup cocoa, 2 tsp baking powder/soda, 1 tsp salt, 2 eggs, 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup oil, 2 tsp vanilla, and 1 cup boiling water. Mix ingredients, stir in water, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 cup buttermilk or milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract.

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Mix Dry: In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  3. Mix Wet: Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes.
  4. Combine: Stir in boiling water last (batter will be thin).
  5. Bake: Pour evenly into pans. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a wooden toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
